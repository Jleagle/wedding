FAQs

Where is the ceremony and reception taking place?

Both will be taking place at Burley Manor in the New Forest

What is the timeline for the day?

Day guests should arrive at Burley Manor for 2:00pm. Evening guests should arrive from 7:30pm.

I have special dietary requirements, how do I let you know?

Please include any special dietary requirements on the RSVP form.

How do I get home from Burley Manor?

Please ensure you have arranged transport for carriages at 11:00pm. Burley Manor is in the New Forest countryside, therefore on demand taxies are highly limited. We have included a number of taxi services on our local amenities page.

Are you inviting children?

We will be inviting children but due to venue restrictions we unfortunately cannot invite everyone - if invited we will include their invitation on your invite. We hope this advance notice means you’re able to let your hair down and celebrate with us on the day (exception for children under 12 months)

Can I invite a plus one?

Unfortunately, due to venue restrictions, we are unable to accommodate plus ones. We appreciate your understanding.

Do you have a gift registry?

Please know that your love and company to help us celebrate our wedding day is the only thing we could ask for! However, if you do wish to celebrate with a gift, a contribution towards our honeymoon would be much appreciated.

Do you have a dresscode?

Our theme is semiformal or garden party. Although our wedding is in August we know the UK summer can be unpredictable so please consider bringing additional layers for the evening. The most of important thing is that you’re comfortable and confident with what you’re wearing.

What’s the bar situation?

Selected drinks will be provided and available during the drinks reception and dinner service. Should you fancy something different there is also a bar available which requires payment (cash and card accepted). Drinks can also be purchased from here for the evening reception.
