FAQs

Where is the ceremony and reception taking place?

Both will be taking place at Burley Manor

What is the timeline for the day?

Day guests should arrive at Farbridge for 1:30pm. Evening guests should arrive from 7:30pm.

I have special dietary requirements, how do I let you know?

Please include any special dietary requirements on the RSVP form.

How do I get home from Farbridge?

Please ensure you have arranged transport for carriages at 11:30. Farbridge is in the countryside, therefore on demand taxi’s are highly limited. We have included a number of taxi services on our local amenities page.

Are you inviting children?

As much as we love your little ones, this will be an adults-only celebration. We hope this advance notice means you’re able to let your hair down and celebrate with us on the day (exception for children under 12 months)

Can I invite a plus one?

Unfortunately, due to venue restrictions, we are unable to accommodate plus ones. We appreciate your understanding.

Do you have a gift registry?

Please know that your love and company on our wedding day is the only thing we could ask for! However, if you do wish to celebrate with a gift, a contribution towards our honeymoon would be much appreciated.

Do you have a dresscode?

Our theme is semi-formal or cocktail. Given our wedding is in October please consider bringing additional layers for the evening (English weather is unpredictable!). The most of important thing is that you’re comfortable and confident with what you’re wearing.

What’s the bar situation?

Specially selected drinks will be provided and available throughout the drinks reception, speeches and dinner service (Thanks Wines by the Sea!). From 8pm onwards guests will be asked to pay for drinks from the bar (although if there’s a small amount left of the free stuff, they might be kind enough to let you have a tipple)
